Editor mode tutorial!

Learn how to create new collidables

  1. CLICK on Create new collidables button. You will now see the text You are now creating new collidables
  2. LEFT CLICK where you want your new collidable to start. You will see a green circle on the starting position.
  3. LEFT CLICK where you want your new collidable to end. You will see the collidable.
  4. Repeat the process for new collidables or EXIT by clicking on You are now creating new collidables.

Learn how to delete collidables

  1. LEFT CLICK on any collidable. You should now see the collidable in green. Make sure you are not creating collidables.
  2. LEFT CLICK anywhere if you don't want to delete that collidable.
  3. RIGHT CLICK anywhere to delete the collidable.
